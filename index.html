Microservices Day London

May 10th, 2016 / The Barbican Centre, London


REGISTER SOON!

Digital Transformation with Microservices

Following on from the success of the New York event, Microservices Day is coming to the Barbican, Europe’s largest arts events venue, in the City of London.

The day is focused on the business benefits of utilising Microservices, including rapid development, rapid deployment and reduced costs. Learn how leading companies are transforming how they do business.

Get real advice and insights on the following areas:

  • Digital Transformation using Microservices & Continuous Delivery
  • From Monolith to New Deployment in 12 weeks
  • Running & Integrating MicroServices on Legacy platforms
  • Using Microservices as a migration technology

Hear how leading companies are delivering increased revenues and reduced costs with microservices and other technologies.
